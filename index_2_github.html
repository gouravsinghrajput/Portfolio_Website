About Me

I MAKE
MACHINES
SEE.

Hey! I'm Gourav — a Computer Vision nerd who genuinely thinks it's wild that you can take a bunch of pixels and make a computer understand what's in them. Studying B.Tech E&TC at SGSITS, Indore — but honestly, my real classroom is my terminal running OpenCV at 30fps.

My thing is building real-time vision systems — hand tracking, pose estimation, gesture interfaces, object detection with YOLO. If it involves a camera feed and Python, I'm obsessed. I also do web dev for when I need something pretty on a screen that isn't a live video feed.

Also into digital design, art, and making things look ✨ — because what's the point of building cool stuff if it looks ugly?
